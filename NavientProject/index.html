Complaints against Navient

By Miles Grovic

Navient has over 5,000 complaints in the past three years, according to the Consumer Finance Protection Bureau’s Consumer Complaint Database. More than 2/3rds (around 3,800) of these complaints are about student loans, including both private and federal loans. Although there are only about 3,800 logged complaints about Nevient’s student loan issues, after losing a recent lawsuit, Nevient is set to give more than 66,000 Navient private student loan borrowers complete loan cancellation on their remaining balances.

The Navient complaints are distributed around the country as one would expect for the most part, with the most amount of complaints coming from the highest populated states such as California and Texas. Some states, such as Georgia, have more complaints proportional to their population. The complaints mainly come from the East coast and the Midwest, with the expected outliers of Texas, California and Washington.
