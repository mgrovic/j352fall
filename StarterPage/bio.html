DC Breeze Fan Page

Bio

Established in 2013, the DC Breeze proudly represent the District of Columbia and National Capital region as they launch their eleventh American Ultimate Disc League (AUDL) season in 2024. The Breeze have earned a playoff berth in the four of the last five seasons and enjoyed the franchise's first postseason victories in 2016 and 2017.The Breeze play at Carlini Field
